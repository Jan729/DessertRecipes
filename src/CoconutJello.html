Coconut Jello

Prep time: 15 mins
Total time: 3 hrs 15 mins
Yield: About 6 cups of jello
This recipe was originally from a very close family friend. I've modified the ingredients a little and improved the instructions so it's even easier to make the jello.

Ingredients:

  • 4 packages Knox gelatin
  • 1 1/2 cups cold water
  • 1 1/2 cups hot water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, pour the cold water over the gelatin and let it sit for a minute while you gather the other ingredients.
  2. Pour in the hot water and sugar. Whisk to dissolve everything.
  3. Whisk in coconut milk, then milk.
  4. Pour into jello mold or 9x13" pan. If pouring into pan, cover the surface of the jello with cling wrap to prevent a hard skin from forming.
  5. Set for about 3 hours in the fridge or until firm.
